Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 8, 2024

Cuadro ("us", "we", or "our") operates this website (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our Service. By using Cuadro, you agree to the collection and use of information as outlined in this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

Cuadro is a free, anonymous service. We do not collect or store personal information from users. Uploaded data is processed temporarily for visualization purposes only and is not retained after the session ends.

2. Non-personal Data Collection

To improve the quality of our Service, we may collect non-personal usage data, such as aggregated analytics, through standard logging practices. This information is collected without cookies or tracking technologies and does not identify individual users.

3. Data Security

We prioritize data security and employ measures such as SSL encryption to protect data in transit. However, please note that no internet transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure, and we cannot fully guarantee data security. Users upload data to the Service at their own risk.

4. Data Retention

Cuadro does not retain or store any data after the user’s session ends. Data uploaded to Cuadro is used only for the duration of the session and is automatically deleted upon completion of the visualization process.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share or disclose any user data to third parties. All data processing is conducted within Cuadro’s environment, with no external sharing or selling of user data.

6. Children's Privacy

Cuadro does not knowingly collect or process data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided data to our Service, please contact us so we can take appropriate action to delete this information.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect any changes in our data handling practices. All updates will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly. We recommend reviewing this page occasionally to stay informed about our practices.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us through the contact form in the Contact section.
